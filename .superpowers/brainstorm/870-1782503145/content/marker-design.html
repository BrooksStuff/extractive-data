Marker Design Systems

Facilities (datacenters, gas plants) vs. entries (effects, testimony). Icons come from /assets/icons/ — SVG placeholders shown.

A — Icon Badge
✓ SELECTED
Facility — sized ring, icon from /assets
Entry — category color, icon from /assets

Icon Badge

Circular marker with SVG icon inside. Facilities get a heavier colored ring, sized by MW. Entries use a thinner ring colored by category. Familiar, map-readable, icons add instant recognition.

B — Crosshair / Survey
✓ SELECTED
Facility — crosshair, outer ring sized by MW
Entry — smaller crosshair, category color

Crosshair / Survey Marker

Engineering survey aesthetic — crosshairs with a center dot, outer ring sized by MW for facilities. No icons; color is the only differentiator. Stark and precise. Reads well at distance; loses detail at small sizes.

C — Icon Pin
✓ SELECTED
Facility — teardrop pin, icon in white
Entry — smaller pin, category color, icon in white

Icon Pin

Classic map pin (teardrop) with SVG icon in the head. Facilities larger, entries smaller. Icon visible at a glance. Most familiar mapping convention — highest out-of-the-box readability.
